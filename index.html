about

Hi, I'm Scoti. I'm a full stack developer with a passion for creating meaningful user experiences. I love brainstorming innovative ways we can use technology to solve people problems. One small step for JavaScript, one giant leap for mankind?

Prior to programming, I spent 5 years in the legal industry doing everything but law - namely, event management, excel wizardry, and process improvement projects. All of these roles were people focused and I've brought that people-first mentality with me to the tech world. (You might be surprised to find out how similar designing an app can feel to designing an event!)

When I'm not coding, I love going to comedy shows, enjoying live music, and using my computer for other creative pursuits - writing, graphic design, videography. Otherwise, I'm probably snuggling with my cat, Zelda, or my rescue pup, Ruby.
